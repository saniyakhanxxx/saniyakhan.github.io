About Saniya

Saniya is a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) in the state of Texas. She received her Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from the University of Tennessee at Knoxville. She continued her education and earned a Master of Science in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from the University of Memphis, a nationally accredited CACREP program. Saniya has experience working with individuals dealing with Eating Disorders, Anxiety, Depression, Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder, and Substance Abuse Addictions.
