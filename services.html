Services Offered

Eating Disorders

Specialized therapy to help individuals overcome eating disorders such as anorexia, bulimia, and binge eating. Saniya provides a supportive environment to develop healthy eating habits and improve self-image.

Anxiety Management

Effective counseling techniques to manage anxiety, reduce stress, and enhance coping mechanisms. Learn strategies to handle anxiety triggers and lead a calmer life.

Depression Therapy

Compassionate therapy aimed at understanding the root causes of depression. Saniya works with you to develop personalized strategies to improve mood and regain joy in life.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder Support

Supportive counseling for individuals experiencing PTSD. Learn to process traumatic events and reduce symptoms through proven therapeutic methods.

Substance Abuse Counseling

Comprehensive counseling for overcoming addictions to substances. Saniya provides guidance on the path to recovery, focusing on both mental and physical health.
