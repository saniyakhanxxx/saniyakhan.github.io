About Saniya Khan

Saniya is a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) in the state of Texas. She received her Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from the University of Tennessee at Knoxville. She continued her education and earned a Master of Science in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from the University of Memphis, a nationally accredited CACREP program.

With extensive experience in treating Eating Disorders, Anxiety, Depression, Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder, and Substance Abuse Addictions, Saniya is dedicated to helping individuals navigate their mental health journey. She encourages patients to change their thought patterns to positively influence their behaviors and lifestyle.

Saniya believes in the strong connection between mental and physical health and advocates for improving mental well-being as a foundation for overall health. She is passionate about uplifting others and providing a supportive environment for healing and growth.
